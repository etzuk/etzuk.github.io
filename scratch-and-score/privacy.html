Privacy Policy — Scratch & Score

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Scratch & Score ("the app") is a football guessing game. We respect your privacy. The app has no user accounts and does not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

Information we process

  • Anonymous game ID. To enable the head-to-head challenge, the app uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication, which creates a random identifier for your device. It is not linked to your real identity.
  • Game progress. Your level, score and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising & analytics identifiers. If ads are enabled, our advertising partners may process device identifiers and basic usage data to show and measure ads. This is not linked to your identity.

Third-party services

The app may use the following services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Realtime Database) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta Audience Network — facebook.com/about/privacy

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and is not directed at collecting personal information from children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your choices

You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking in your device settings. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data.
